Consultation

The objective of the consultation is to offer a specialized study to improve the quality of the vital energy in the environment, to create balance, wellbeing and harmony, which brings peace and happiness to people.

Services included in the study:

  • * 3 visits at least to the agreed place to take photographs and make an assessment of the energy status of the spaces
  • * Making a diagnosis with all the relevant details regarding the treatment that corresponds in each area
  • * Make a presentation in pdf file with the design, images and maps with the orientations by areas to apply it in the points that have to be improved or adjusted for a better balance.
  • * Demostrate in the analysisthe well balance areas and unbalanced areas and the conclusions to be followed.
  • * The recommendations of the relocation of existing furniture and the decoration suggestions according to feng shui, in general with colors, shapes and styles.
  • * The delivery of the Feng Shui study will be personalized and in the hands of the client. The study will be explained in detail verbally on the day of delivery, which will be at least 3 to 8 weeks after hiring the services which , depends on the size and characteristics.
  • * The cost of the study depends on the place.A 50% down payment is required and the remaining 50% on the day of the study delivery
